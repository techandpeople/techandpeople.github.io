Tech and People

Somos um grupo de investigação da Faculdade de Ciências, LASIGE. Dedicado a criar tecnologias para, e com pessoas e temos muito interesse em colaborar e perceber as vossas necessidades.

Objetivo

O nosso principal objetivo é tornar a tecnologia útil para promover a inclusão de diversos backgrounds e capacidades (sensoriais, cognitivas, culturais, nacionalidades) promovendo uma sociedade mais diversa e inclusiva.

Somos um grupo focado em interação pessoa-máquina para a inclusão e acessibilidade de pessoas com e sem deficiência ou com diferentes habilidades e oportunidades.

Usamos diversas tecnologias

Inclusive Gaming

By acknowledging that players have different needs and preferences, we explore strategies to create gaming experiences that are more engaging and inclusive to everyone! We explore solutions to increase the agency of individual users over the interfaces they use.

Personalized Digital Interventions

We seek to tailor and personalize digital interventions to increase users motivation and adherence to the interventions. To this end, is crucial to gather a deep understanding of the intervention, context of use, benefits and difficulties associated and to accede to the mental models (goals, needs, motivations, etc) of the users.

HRI for Health and Acessibility

Robots have a very particular set of skills. Skills that have been acquired over a vast amount of reasearch. Skills that make them powerful tools to leverage for social inclusion, accessibility and interaction.

Personalization of User Interfaces

We explore solutions to increase the agency of individual users over the interfaces they use. We envision a digital world were peole have the power to adjust user interfaces (UI) to their personal interests and needs.

Data-Driven Healthcare

Our goal is to increase and improve the information clinicians and patients have access. We explore and develop mechanisms for collecting, processing, and analyzing the data. We also have a huge concern on how the information is presented.

Inclusive Education

We aim to develop technological solutions to enable an inclusive education for children with mixed abilities. We have mainly developed enhanced tangible objects for the acquisition of abstract concepts, such as mathematics or computational thinking. Besides the benefit of decreasing cognitive load and reinforcing understanding, manipulatives also support collaboration, making children more prone to divide, explore and share supporting cooperative actions.

Smartphone Accessibility

From newcomers to experts, we have characterized the challenges that blind people overcome. We explored how to provide effective self-contained smartphone assistance with the ultimate goal of promoting self-efficacy.

Biographical and Cognitive Stimulation

We explore how biographical information can improve cognitive stimulation and design interactive platforms to support people with dementia, clinicians, and families.

Como nos podem ajudar?

O nosso objetivo é desenvolver tecnologia para problemas reais, e trabalhar com e para a sociedade. Assim sendo temos sempre diversos projetos de investigação a decorrer.

Caso tenha interesse pode ajudar-nos em diversas fases dos projetos, desde a identificação das vossas necessidades que a tecnologia pode ajudar, até desenhar e testar os protótipos/soluções que a nossa equipa vai desenvolvendo.
